## Wild & Temporary Cards

**<span style="color:#ccc">Wild Cards (W cost)</span>** can be paid with any color mana. Examples: _Bracelet_ (Attack, 100 dmg), _Parm Aegis_ (Defense, +1 Armor), _Wings_ (Mana, reduce next cost by 1).

**<span style="color:#888">Temporary Cards</span>** appear during specific encounters or events. Usually black-bordered debuffs or one-time effects. Not collectible.
