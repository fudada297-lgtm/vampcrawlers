All **22 playable characters** — purchased at the **Gorton Bell Inn** in the Village. Each adds cards to your deck and a unique trigger effect.

:::caution[How Characters Work]
Your **leading character** (first slot) adds their stats + 4 character cards to your starter deck. Additional characters (slots 2–5) add only 1 card each. Choose your leader based on the trigger color that matches your deck's strategy.
:::

:::tip[Synergy Tip]
Match your leader's trigger color to your deck's primary card type. Antonio (+10% dmg on red) pairs with attack-heavy decks. Arca (+1 mana on purple) powers mana-hungry spell builds. Christine (Disarm on purple) is excellent for control decks.
:::
