All **15 relics** — permanent upgrades hidden in dungeons. Once collected, they stay active forever (can be toggled in the Museum).

:::caution[How to find relics]
Destroy breakable objects (barrels, crates, torches) in dungeons. Each relic is in a **specific dungeon**. The _Guiding Light_ relic (from Inlaid Library) highlights destructibles on your mini-map — get this early.
:::
